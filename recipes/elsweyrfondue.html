Elsweyr Fondue

What is Elsweyr Fondue

Elsweyr fondue packs a pound of Magicka to fill up your gut, so it can be useful for those long travels through the wilderness fighting the native creatures on the way. You might even be lucky enough to find all your ingredients at your local caravan! Though the Empire deems it illegal, you are not punished for owning it or selling it to vendors across Skyrim.

Ingredients

  • Ale
  • Elder Cheese Wheel
  • Moon Sugar

How to cook

  1. Taste ale
  2. Throw all ingredients into large pot
  3. Place pot above the fire and simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Don't get caught eating this delicacy
